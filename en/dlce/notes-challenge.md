# Notes Challenge

> Gameplay

## Rules

<blockquote>

- In this event, your mission is to collect every diamond and crown, perfectly pass the level with just one cube, and remember: no revives allowed! Are you up for the challenge? 
- Guidance Lines are turned off during gameplay, so use your own skills!
- Once you’ve perfected the level, don’t forget to head back to the event page to claim your rewards!

</blockquote>

## Completion Rewards
- Level 1: 1 Note
- Level 2: 2 Notes
- Level 3: 3 Notes
- Level 4: 4 Notes
- Total: 10 Notes
